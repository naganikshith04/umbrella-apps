About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by height in meters squared.

Formula: BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]Â²

Note: BMI is a screening tool and does not diagnose body fatness or health. Consult healthcare professionals for personalized advice.
