# Auto Loan Calculator

Calculate car loan payments with trade-in value, down payment, and total cost of ownership

## Features

- Monthly payment with APR and loan term
- Trade-in value and down payment inputs
- Sales tax and fees calculator
- Total cost of ownership over loan term
- Lease vs buy comparison option
