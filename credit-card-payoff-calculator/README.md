# Credit Card Payoff Calculator

Calculate how long to pay off credit card debt and compare minimum payment vs fixed payment strategies

## Features

- Multiple credit card tracking (up to 10 cards)
- Minimum payment vs fixed payment comparison
- Avalanche and snowball method calculators
- Interest savings visualization
- Debt-free date projection
