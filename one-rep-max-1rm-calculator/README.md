# One Rep Max (1RM) Calculator

Calculate your maximum lifting capacity and generate training percentages

## Features

- Multiple 1RM formulas (Epley, Brzycki, Lombardi, etc.)
- Training percentage chart (60%-95% of 1RM)
- Rep range recommendations for different goals
- Progress tracking comparison
- Exercise-specific calculations for major lifts

## Keywords

one rep max calculator, 1rm calculator, max lift calculator, bench press calculator, squat max calculator, strength calculator
