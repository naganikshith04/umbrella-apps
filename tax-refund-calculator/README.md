# Tax Refund Calculator

Estimate federal and state tax refund or amount owed based on income, deductions, and withholdings

## Features

- W-2 income and withholding inputs
- Standard vs itemized deduction comparison
- Tax credit eligibility (EITC, Child Tax Credit, etc.)
- State tax calculation for all 50 states
- Refund or payment estimate

## Keywords

tax refund calculator, tax calculator, income tax calculator, federal tax calculator, tax return calculator, tax estimator, irs refund calculator, tax withholding calculator

