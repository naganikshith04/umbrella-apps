# Rent vs Buy Calculator

Compare the true cost of renting versus buying a home over time with investment opportunity costs

## Features

- 5, 10, 15, 30 year comparison scenarios
- Opportunity cost of down payment invested
- Home appreciation and rent increase assumptions
- Tax benefit calculations (mortgage interest deduction)
- Break-even point analysis

## Keywords

rent vs buy calculator, rent or buy calculator, buying vs renting calculator, should i rent or buy, rent vs own calculator, home buying calculator, rent vs mortgage calculator, cost of buying vs renting
